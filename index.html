About Us

Over 35 Years Experience in This Industry


Established in 1990, Ahmad Builders is a trusted name in Pakistan’s construction and wood industry. We proudly work with Frontier Works Organization (FWO) and the *Pakistan Army, undertaking a wide range of road and building construction projects across the country. Our dedication to quality, precision, and reliability has earned us long-term partnerships and recognition in both public and private sectors.

In addition to construction, we are also leading importers of premium wood from the USA and other countries, supplying high-quality materials for various uses, including doors, furniture, and interior products. With decades of experience and a commitment to excellence, Ahmad Builders continues to grow as a symbol of strength, craftsmanship, and trust in both national and international markets.
